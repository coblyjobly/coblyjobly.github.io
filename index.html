Swap Dash

January 2021

In Swap Dash, you take on the role of a clever raccoon determined to retrieve your family's secret sandwich recipe from a high-tech sandwich factory. This fast-paced puzzle game immerses you in a series of challenging levels, where you must navigate the factory's intricate layout.

Equipped with a unique topping gun, you can shoot switches that rearrange the factory's environment, enabling you to manipulate the layout to your advantage. Strategy and quick thinking are essential as you solve puzzles to progress through each level and ultimately reclaim the cherished recipe. Made with GameMaker.
