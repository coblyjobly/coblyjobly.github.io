About Me

Hello! My name is Colby, and I am a senior at Florida State University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Innovation. With a strong GPA of 3.6, I am deeply passionate about software development, game design, and environmental initiatives. My journey in game development began in high school, where I first started creating games, and this passion has driven me to pursue programming with the goal of crafting immersive experiences that captivate and engage players.

Currently, I am diversifying my skill set through application development, which allows me to broaden my expertise beyond gaming. I actively enhance my skills through my involvement in student organizations and internships. As a Manager at New World Rides, I developed valuable troubleshooting and operational skills while creating a fun atmosphere for guests in a fast-paced environment. My role as Project Facilitator at the FSU Innovation Hub allows me to manage projects, mentor peers, and engage in innovative problem-solving through web development. Additionally, my experience as a Technical Support Consultant for Art District Real Estate has further honed my ability to work collaboratively and think creatively, enabling me to bring unique perspectives to my projects.

Swap Dash

January 2021

In Swap Dash, you take on the role of a clever raccoon determined to retrieve your family's secret sandwich recipe from a high-tech sandwich factory. This fast-paced puzzle game immerses you in a series of challenging levels, where you must navigate the factory's intricate layout.

Equipped with a unique topping gun, you can shoot switches that rearrange the factory's environment, enabling you to manipulate the layout to your advantage. Strategy and quick thinking are essential as you solve puzzles to progress through each level and ultimately reclaim the cherished recipe. Made with GameMaker.

Contributions: Project Management, Game Design, Sprite Art, Sound Design, Game Maker Language(GML)

Fatal Business: Legendary Adventure

January 2020

**Fatal Business** is a beat 'em up game created for the Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) to highlight their programs. Players take on the role of Mr. Business as he travels to different global conferences. Along the way, he battles evil business bots, representing various challenges in the business world. The game combines fun action with lessons on leadership and teamwork, making it an entertaining way to learn about the values of FBLA. Made with Unity.
