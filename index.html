Swap Dash

January 2021

In Swap Dash, you take on the role of a raccoon determined to retrieve your family's secret sandwich recipe from a evil sandwich factory. This fast-paced puzzle game immerses you in three levels, where you must navigate the factory's intricate layout. Equipped with a unique topping gun, you will shoot switches that alter the factory's environment, enabling you to manipulate the layout to your advantage. Strategy and quick thinking are essential as you solve puzzles to progress through each level and ultimately reclaim the family recipe. Made with GameMaker.

January 2020

Fatal Business: Legendary Adventure is a beat 'em up game created for the Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) to highlight their programs.
